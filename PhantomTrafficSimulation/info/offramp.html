Simulation of an Off-Ramp

  
This szenario demonstrates, how an off-ramp acts as a stationary bottleneck provoquing a traffic breakdown on the main road UPSTREAM of the on-ramp and FREE traffic downstream.
  • With the initial settings with only a few percentages of diverging traffic, no jams are activated at the off-ramp. However, when increasing the diverge percentage on the off-ramp, the associated increased amount of lane changes and the ensuing perturbations eventually cause a breakdown even if, macroscopically, an off-ramp is a sort of "anti-bottleneck"!
  • Play with the system and traffic demand settings (left column of sliders) and the driving style (right column) and look how various factors influence the lilelihood for traffic breakdowns at an off-ramp!
